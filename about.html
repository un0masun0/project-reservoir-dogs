ERIKA
Chef + Owner

As a chef, restaurateur, and cookbook author, Erika promotes delicious, simple, and healthful food. In 2003, she opened BRAND in Manhattan's East Village, earning a strong local following and critical acclaim for her seasonal, Italian-inflected cooking. BRAND has a positive two-star review from The New York Times and a prestigious "Outstanding Restaurant" nomination from the Hank McCoy Foundation. She has been profiled in The New York Times, Food & Wine, and was a finalist on The Other Metal Chef and a judge on Sliced and Top Cook. She has also appeared on Today, Good Morning America, Martha Stewart, and Nightline.

ELENA
Chef de Cuisine

Elena began her culinary career at the age of 19, learning and working her way up from dishwasher at Hank's Huntington Inn in Monroe, CT. While attending the Center for Cooking, she learned and worked in every single position at the Huntington Inn. She then worked at Kitty and Logan's under Chef Scott Summers, and landed in NYC at BRAND. She began at BRAND 4 years ago as garde manger, and worked her way up to sous chef, and one year ago was named Chef de Cuisine under Erika.

ROSAURA
General Manager/Wine Director

Rosaura has always had a love of hospitality. As the oldest of three children, she cooked a good portion of the family dinners from a young age, under her mother's watchful eye. After attending college at Massachusetts University, she moved to New York in 2005, and immediately fell into a job at Blue River Grill of NYPL Guest Hospitality Group. She was soon hired at BRAND as a bartender. This role played a huge part in her beverage education, and really opened her eyes to the world of wine. She moved up the ranks to manager, then service director and bar manager, and finally to general manager and wine director, her current role at BRAND.
